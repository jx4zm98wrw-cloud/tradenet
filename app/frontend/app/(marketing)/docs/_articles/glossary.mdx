<DocsH2 num="02" id="g-issues">Gazette issue conventions</DocsH2>

Vietnam IP publishes a weekly bulletin in two parallel streams. Filenames follow a strict convention: <span className="mono">{`{SECTION}_{ISSUE}_{YEAR}.pdf`}</span>.

<DocsCallout heading="Application numbers">
  Vietnamese app numbers follow <span className="mono">4-YYYY-NNNNN</span>. The leading “4” is the IP-right type (4 = trademark). Certificate numbers (granted marks) follow <span className="mono">4-NNNNNNN</span> with no year.
</DocsCallout>
